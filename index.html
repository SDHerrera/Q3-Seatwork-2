Blossom

Blossom is the intelligent and determined leader of the Powerpuff Girls. She is known for her pink color and her ice breath.

Bubbles

Bubbles is the cute and sensitive member of the Powerpuff Girls. She is characterized by her blue color and her ability to speak to animals.

Buttercup

Buttercup is the tough and tomboyish member of the Powerpuff Girls. She is identified by her green color and her super strength.
