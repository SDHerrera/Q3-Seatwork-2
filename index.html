Blossom

Blossom is the intelligent and determined leader of the Powerpuff Girls. She is known for her pink color and her ice breath.

Bubbles

Bubbles is the cute and sensitive member of the Powerpuff Girls. She is characterized by her blue color and her ability to speak to animals. She often wears a blue dress and has blonde pigtails.

Buttercup

Buttercup is the tough and tomboyish member of the Powerpuff Girls. She is identified by her green color and her super strength. She is known for her short black hair and green dress. She enjoys sports and is very competitive.

Character Details

Name Description
Blossom Blossom is the intelligent and determined leader of the Powerpuff Girls. She is known for her pink color and her ice breath. She is the self-proclaimed "Commander and the Leader" of the Powerpuff Girls.
Bubbles Bubbles is the cute and sensitive member of the Powerpuff Girls. She is characterized by her blue color and her ability to speak to animals. She often wears a blue dress and has blonde pigtails. Bubbles is the "Joy and the Laughter" of the Powerpuff Girls.
Buttercup Buttercup is the tough and tomboyish member of the Powerpuff Girls. She is identified by her green color and her super strength. She is known for her short black hair and green dress. She enjoys sports and is very competitive. Buttercup is the "Toughest Fighter" of the Powerpuff Girls.
